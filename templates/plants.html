Plants Database - Nature's Doctors

Here we can learn about plants and why we called them Nature's Doctors.
They can help not only humans, but assist the whole environment.

  • They can improve or rebuild soil life.
  • They help and support other species through nutrients.
  • They attract beneficial insects to our gardens.
  • Some species of plants can act as natural pesticides.
  • They are used to treat or heal various human symptoms.
